Members Benefits

We have different membership categories each with it's own benefits. Interested parties can either register with us then join different membership categories.

Below are some of the membership Categories you can join upon registration.

Benefits to registered Farmers

Registered farmers get an access to our various premium services including extension services, quality seeds, modern technology, networking with other farmers, sales and marketing for farm products. Register Now

Benefits to registered Students

We help students registered with us secure attachments/internship positions in our numerous partner organizations and idustries. We also advise them on how to create jobs and employment among themselves

Other categories include:
  • Consultants
    • Create job opportunity; link them to clients, opportunity for research
  • Entrepreneurs
    • Will be linked to clients both suppliers and buyers, financial services, opportunities in the market.
  • Consumers
    • Will be linked to suppliers or sellers, advise on price trend in the market, quality goods and services
  • Institutions
    • Education for sustainable development, Income development projects, offer research centres, link to the community, increase student population, advise students on matters pertaining education, discipline, job opportunities, attachments
  • Industries
    • Will be linked to suppliers and consumers, market research, consumer/suppliers feedback
